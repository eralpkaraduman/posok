NullPoint is an space adventure, rpg, sidescroller shooter game, with bright sharp minimalistic 8bit graphics and sounds. Explore the vast deep space. Trade with countless space stations and planets in numerous galaxies

Game is currently under heavy development, it's considered to be deployed to as many as possible patforms, web, iphone, android, pc, mac, xbox, psn, wii. Flash/web prototype is going fast on development, you may be playing first alpha in following weeks!
